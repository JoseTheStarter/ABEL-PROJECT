Triumph Rocket 3

The Triumph Rocket 3 boasts a large 2,458cc triple engine, known for its impressive torque and acceleration. It features a muscular design and is available in two variants: the Rocket 3 Storm R and the Rocket 3 Storm GT.

Specifications

  • Engine: 2458cc liquid-cooled 12-valve DOHC inline 3-cylinder
  • Power: 165 bhp @ 6000 rpm
  • Torque: 221 Nm @ 4000 rpm
  • Transmission: 6-speed with slip-assist clutch
  • Fuel Capacity: 18 liters
  • Weight: 291 kg (Storm R) / 294 kg (Storm GT)
